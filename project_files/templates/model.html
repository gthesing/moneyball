Results

The linear model was too simple and was too simple and resulted in an MSE > .60 and an r2 < .35.

The sequential model was too complex and was overfitting the model very quickly because of our limited data set. We were able to get this model's loss down to .22 and the accuracy to .89, however it required reducing the number of layers to 1 and the number of nodes to 10.

The lgoistic regerssion model seemed to be the best fit and we achieved an MSE < .14 and an r2 > .86.
